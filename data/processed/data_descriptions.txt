DIM_CALENDAR.csv

WEEK: Human-friendly identifier comining the week number and year
YEAR: Structured year listing
MONTH: Structured month listing
WEEK_NUMBER: Structured list of weeks of the year for aligning with sales cycles
DATE: Start date that represents the given week (Monday)
